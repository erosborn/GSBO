Smörgåstårta (Swedish Sandwich Cake)

Ingredients:

Base:

  • 24 slices white bread

Filling 1:

  • 3 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 150 g smoked salmon
  • 50 g arugula

Filling 2:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill or chives

Filling 3:

  • 125 g cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 200 g peeled shrimp
  • 50 g arugula

Topping:

  • 250 g cream cheese
  • 4 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 radishes
  • ½ lemon
  • 5 slices smoked salmon
  • 100 g peeled shrimp
  • Fresh dill

Instructions:

Assemble the Base and First Filling:

  1. Cut off the crusts of the bread slices. Arrange six slices in a rectangle on a platter.
  2. Mix crème fraîche and Dijon mustard, then spread evenly over the bread.
  3. Layer smoked salmon evenly on top, followed by arugula.
  4. Add another layer of six bread slices on top.

Prepare the Second Filling:

  1. Peel and mash the boiled eggs in a bowl.
  2. Mix with crème fraîche, mayonnaise, mustard, and dill (or chives).
  3. Spread evenly over the bread layer.
  4. Add six more slices of bread on top, pressing down slightly.

Prepare the Third Filling:

  1. Mix cream cheese and crème fraîche.
  2. Spread the mixture evenly over the bread.
  3. Top with shrimp and arugula.
  4. Add six more slices of bread on top, pressing down slightly.

Finish and Decorate the Cake:

  1. Mix cream cheese and crème fraîche, then spread evenly over the top and sides of the cake.
  2. Insert a few toothpicks on top to hold plastic wrap in place, then cover and refrigerate for at least one hour.
  3. Slice the boiled egg, cucumber, radishes, and lemon.
  4. Decorate the top with egg slices, smoked salmon, shrimp, cucumber, radishes, and fresh dill. Optional: Garnish the sides with additional dill or arugula.

Preparation Time:

Preparation Time
30 minutes
Resting Time
1 hour
Total Time
1 hour 30 minutes

Yield: Serves 10
