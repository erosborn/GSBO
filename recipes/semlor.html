Swedish Semlor (Fat Tuesday Buns)

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 tsp instant yeast (or 50 g fresh yeast)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp freshly ground cardamom
  • 6 cups flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar (or vanilla extract)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 egg
  • 11 tbsp butter (room temperature)
  • 1 egg (whisked for brushing)

Almond Filling:

  • 1 ½ cups blanched whole almonds
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup milk or cream (or 2 egg whites)

Topping:

  • 2 cups whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

Make the Dough:

  1. Heat the milk until lukewarm.
  2. Dissolve the yeast in the milk. Add sugar, cardamom, vanilla, salt, egg, and two-thirds of the flour. Mix well by hand or with a mixer.
  3. Stir in the softened butter, then gradually mix in the remaining flour while kneading. Adjust flour if needed.
  4. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until elastic. Cover the dough and let it rise until doubled in size, about 40 to 60 minutes.
  5. Roll the dough into a cylinder and cut into 16–24 pieces, depending on the desired size.
  6. Roll each piece into a ball and place on a baking tray. Cover and let rise for 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 435°F (225°C).
  8. Brush each bun with whisked egg.
  9. Bake in the middle of the oven for 10–12 minutes, until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack.

Prepare the Filling:

  1. Blend almonds and sugar in a food processor until a soft marzipan-like consistency forms.
  2. Add milk (or egg whites) and pulse a few more times until incorporated.

Assemble the Semlor:

  1. Cut off the top of each bun.
  2. Pipe or spoon the almond filling (or jam) onto the base of each bun.
  3. Whip the cream with vanilla sugar and add it on top of the almond filling, using a spoon or piping bag.
  4. Place the bun tops back on. Optionally, use a heart-shaped cutter or scissors to modify the shape.
  5. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

Make the Dough:

  1. "Hëëtën de melk!" – Heat the milk until lukewarm.
  2. "Mîxën de yeasty-beasty!" – Mix yeast, milk, sugar, cardamom, vanilla, salt, egg, and most flour.
  3. "Bootér und flööör!" – Add butter and remaining flour while kneading.
  4. "Knøødën de døøgh!" – Knead until elastic, then let rise for an hour. BÖRK BÖRK!
  5. "Røøllen und cøøtten!" – Roll and cut into pieces.
  6. "Bållen de døøgh!" – Make balls and let rise again.
  7. "Høøtën de øøven!" – Heat oven to super hot!
  8. "Påintën mit eggies!" – Paint with egg wash.
  9. "Bøørken in de øøven!" – Bake until golden! BÖRK!

Prepare the Filling:

  1. "Blendën de nøøts!" – Blend those almonds with sugar until smooth!
  2. "Splåshen de melk!" – Add milk and blend more! WHIRRY WHIRRY!

Assemble the Semlor:

  1. "Chøppen de tøps!" – Cut off those bun tops!
  2. "Squîîrten de fîîlling!" – Add the almond filling!
  3. "Whîîppen de crêême!" – Whip cream and add on top!
  4. "Pøøtten de håts!" – Put the tops back on!
  5. "Snøøwy snøøwy!" – Make it snow with powdered sugar! BÖRK BÖRK BÖRK!

Preparation Time:

Preparation Time
45 minutes
Cooking Time
10–12 minutes
Resting Time
1 hour 10 minutes
Total Time
2 hours 5 minutes

Yield: 24 buns
